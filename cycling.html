Off road Safari

Experience our exciting day trips in our comfortable 4x4 vehicles. Discover lush forests, beautiful beaches, and mountainous areas of Halkidiki. Capture photos, enjoy breaks, and savor a tasty lunch at a traditional taverna. Your safety and the environment are our priorities. Join us for unforgettable tours and excellent service. Don't miss out on Halkidiki's hidden treasures!

    Itinerary

  • Pick up from the hotel is around 8 am
  • Arrive at Kassandrino village. Explore central square, oldest church, and admire beautiful pine trees
  • Proceed to "Mayrobara" turtle lake, a rare and ecologically significant site, recognized as a nature monument since 1998
  • Drive through mountain tops with photo stops, ending at Agia Paraskeyi's lovely rocky beach
  • Continue to Loutra for a brief stop, then arrive at Siviri for lunch and/or swimming
  • The guided excursion typically lasts around 5 hours
