Romantic Dinner

This romantic cruise departs daily at 17:30 from Aliki port and lasts 3 hours. Perfect for celebrating a honeymoon or creating special memories in Greece. Enjoy quality time with loved ones on a traditional wooden boat, feeling the gentle breeze at sunset. Experience the stunning orange skies of the Greek Archipelago in complete privacy, followed by a luxurious dinner. Our dinner features fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Enjoy Parian gastronomy with seafood, seasonal vegetables and fruits, regional mezedes, sweets, salads, and main courses. Mrs. Stavroula, the captain's mother, prepares part of the menu using her secret family recipes, with the rest prepared in front of you.
