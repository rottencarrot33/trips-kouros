Iraklia and
Koufonissi

Sail from Paros to the Lesser Cyclades and relax on the tiny island of Iraklia for 1.5 hours. Enjoy swimming, walking around, and exploring Agios Georgios port. Next, visit Ano Koufonissi, a tranquil island with beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters. Stroll through Hora's narrow alleys, admire the whitewashed houses with blue windows, and visit the beachside library. Explore the island's treasures and have lunch at a traditional fish tavern. From the harbor, take a sea taxi to the nearby uninhabited Kato Koufonissi to explore more beaches.

    Itinerary

  • Starts at 10am from Piso Livari port
  • Enjoy the beautiful natural landscapes of Iraklia
  • Sail to the island of Ano Koufonissi to explore its unique beaches

    Details

  • Onboard guide
  • The guided excursion typically lasts around 8 hours
