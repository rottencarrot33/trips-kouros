Horse Riding

Enjoy a fantastic horse riding excursion for both new and experienced riders with qualified guides. This beach ride will be a highlight of your stay in Paros. Starting in Kormos, just behind Paroikia, you'll ride through scenic Kakapetra and stop at Parasporos beach to admire a breathtaking sunrise. Continue through the luxurious areas of Aspries and Xero Xylo, with stunning views of Parikia Bay and the Aegean Sea. Upon returning, relax and watch the horses being cared for. Share your experience over coffee and cookies, making for a perfect end to this unforgettable tour.

    Itinerary

  • Starts at 6am (Αννα πες μου που είναι το meeting point)
  • Ride through the picturesque area of Kakapetra
  • Make a stop at Parasporos beach to admire the sunrise in a dreamy setting.
  • Pass through the luxurious areas of Aspries and Xero Xylo.

    Details

  • The horse riding typically lasts around 2 hours
