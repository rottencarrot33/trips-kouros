Cooking Lessons

Discover the unique flavors of the Cyclades and learn traditional Greek cooking secrets from Yiayia (grandmother) of Parian cuisine. Start with a little aperitif before preparing a 6-course traditional meal: a starter, a salad, three main dishes (one vegetarian, one seafood, one meat), and a dessert. After cooking, enjoy the meal together with wine or ouzo and fresh garden fruits. Mrs. Anezina will share her grandmother's recipes with you. The experience includes both cooking and eating. Bon appétit and cheers!
