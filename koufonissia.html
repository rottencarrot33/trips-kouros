Koufonissia

Take an unforgettable day trip from Paros to the beautiful island of Koufonissi. Depart from Piso Livadi, Paros at 09:10 am, and after a quick stop in Naxos, you'll arrive in Koufonissi by 11:00 am. This island, part of the Small Cyclades, is famous for its clear turquoise waters and stunning beaches. Enjoy 6 hours exploring the island. Walk through Chora, admiring the white houses with blue windows. You can rent a bike to find hidden spots along scenic trails or take a boat tour to visit various beautiful beaches, perfect for swimming and relaxing.
