Antiparos and
Despotiko

Embark on an unforgettable adventure aboard a traditional wooden fisherman motorboat, sailing around Antiparos and Despotiko. Depart from Pounda port, Paros at 10am. Experience the Aegean Sea's beauty as you sail through clear waters, visiting beautiful beaches, sea caves, and small islands. This cruise lets you explore untouched nature and places only accessible by boat.

    Itinerary

  • Starts at 10am from Pounda port
  • Explore Antiparos and Despotiko
  • Discover hidden gems and secluded beaches
