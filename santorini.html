Santorini

After arriving at Athinio port, our bus and multilingual guide will be ready for you. First, visit Oia, where narrow alleys offer stunning views of whitewashed houses on a cliff. Attractions include the Byzantine castle ruins, Oia Art Gallery, Cape Columbo, and Domaine Sigalas. Next, stop in Fira, Santorini's capital, known for its natural beauty and cosmopolitan vibe. Explore museums and galleries like the Archaeological Museum of Thera and the island's largest shopping center.
