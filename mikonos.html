Mykonos

Visit Mykonos, the "island of the winds," known for its cosmopolitan life and charming whitewashed houses, chapels, and windmills. In Hora, Mykonos town, enjoy the famous "Little Venice" with its unique views of windmills and the sea. Stroll through narrow, cobbled alleys adorned with bougainvilleas and explore premium shops and trendy stores on Matoyannia Street. Rent a motorbike to visit nearby beaches or enjoy a delicious lunch at a traditional tavern. Walk up the hill for a close look at the windmills and a panoramic view. Don't miss the Aegean Maritime Museum and the Orthodox Church of Paraportiani.
